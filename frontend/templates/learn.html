Everything you need to know about evaluating a vendor

Sourcing a supplier isn't just "find a cheap quote." A good evaluation weighs nine dimensions — exactly what VendorScout's agent scores for you, automatically, from live marketplace data.

The 9 dimensions of a solid vendor decision

1 · Relevance

Does the product actually match your spec, quantity and use-case?

2 · Compliance

ISO / CE / industry certifications — claimed vs verifiable.

3 · Financial health

Will they still be around to fulfil a bulk order?

4 · Risk

Negative news, disputes, supply-chain red flags.

5 · Authenticity

BIS/ISI, GST/registration, TrustSEAL — is the seller real?

6 · Reputation

Reviews, ratings, years in business, repeat buyers.

7 · Capability

Capacity & lead time for YOUR volume (e.g. 12 units, bulk).

8 · Price

Quote vs the market index — competitive, average or premium.

9 · Specification

Completeness of technical detail — fewer surprises later.
